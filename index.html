Hospice care is a specialized type of compassionate, patient-centered care designed for individuals who are facing a life-limiting illness with a prognosis of six months or less, as determined by a physician. Rather than focusing on curative treatments, hospice care prioritizes comfort, quality of life, pain management, and emotional and spiritual well-being. At End Of Life Care Thousand Oaks, eligibility is determined through a personalized assessment. If you or a loved one has been diagnosed with a terminal illness and curative treatment is no longer the primary goal, you may qualify for our hospice services. We encourage you to call us anytime — our team is available 24/7 to walk you through the process and answer all your questions.
Hospice care can be provided in a variety of settings based on the patient's needs and the family's preferences. At End Of Life Care Thousand Oaks, we bring our caring hospice services directly to where the patient is most comfortable — whether that is at home, in an assisted living facility, a nursing home, or an inpatient hospice facility. Our goal is to minimize disruption and allow patients to spend their final days in familiar and peaceful surroundings, surrounded by the people and things they love most. During your initial consultation, we will work with you to determine the most suitable care setting for your loved one.
